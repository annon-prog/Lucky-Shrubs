Our History

Lucky Shrub, nestled in the heart of Tucson, Arizona, has grown from the humble beginnings of a shared love for plants by its founders, Jason and Maria. Their journey began as a husband and wife team with a passion for transforming outdoor spaces into vibrant, green oases. Jason, the garden architect, envisioned a company that combined artistry and horticulture. Today, their dream has flourished into a medium-sized garden design firm that is a testament to dedication and vision. With Jason's expertise in garden design and Maria's marketing acumen, they've cultivated Lucky Shrub into a thriving business that offers a holistic approach to garden design, landscaping, and plant nurturing.

Landscaping Services

Lucky Shrub specializes in an array of landscaping services that cater to the diverse needs of its clients. Under the expert guidance of garden architect Jason, the landscaping team transforms ordinary spaces into exquisite gardens. From the initial design concepts to the final installation, Lucky Shrub's landscaping services encompass everything from hardscape design to planting and irrigation systems. Whether it's a tranquil backyard oasis, a sustainable xeriscape, or a vibrant floral garden, they tailor their services to meet the unique preferences and requirements of each client. With a commitment to environmental sustainability, they also offer eco-friendly landscaping solutions, making them a top choice for those seeking both beauty and eco-consciousness in their outdoor spaces.

Plant Nursery

In addition to their landscaping services, Lucky Shrub runs a charming plant nursery. This nursery is a haven for plant enthusiasts, offering a curated selection of indoor and outdoor plants. Maria, who manages the nursery, ensures that it stocks a wide variety of plants suitable for Tucson's climate, allowing clients to create their own green sanctuaries at home. The nursery provides expert advice, helping customers choose the right plants for their space and offering care tips to keep them thriving. Whether you're looking for succulents, cacti, tropical houseplants, or native desert flora, Lucky Shrub's plant nursery is the go-to destination for plant lovers in Tucson.

Products Offered

Lucky Shrub is more than just a garden design and landscaping company; it's a one-stop shop for all your gardening and landscaping needs. Beyond their landscaping and nursery services, they also offer an assortment of products to assist clients in creating and maintaining their dream gardens. This includes a wide range of gardening tools, soil amendments, irrigation supplies, and decorative elements to enhance the outdoor aesthetic. They take pride in offering a comprehensive selection of products that reflect their commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Whether you're an experienced gardener or just starting out, Lucky Shrub's products and expertise are at your disposal to help transform any space into an oasis you can be proud of.
